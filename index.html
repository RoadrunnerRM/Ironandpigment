What I Offer

Service Tiers

All tiers deliver solid, table-ready results. Choose based on your models and the level of detail you're after.

Tier I

Rank & File

Efficient, clean painting ideal for large units and hordes that need to hit the table quickly without sacrificing quality.

Estimated time for 2000 points: 20–30 hours

  • Thorough assembly cleaning & priming
  • Base colours & contrast/wash technique
  • Drybrushing & basic highlights
  • Basing included (basic textures)

Tier II — Most Popular

Tabletop Standard

The sweet spot. Full basecoat-shade-highlight workflow producing models that look excellent under game conditions and in photos.

Estimated time for 2000 points: 50 hours

  • Everything in Rank & File
  • Layered highlights on key areas
  • Eye and face detail
  • Gem & lens effects
  • Scenic basing option

Tier III

Hero & Centrepiece

For characters, monsters, vehicles, and models that deserve extra attention. Time and craft invested to make them stand out.

Estimated time for 2000 points: 100–120 hours

  • Everything in Tabletop Standard
  • OSL / glow effects where appropriate
  • Weathering & battle damage
  • Freehand designs & banners
  • Photo showcase before dispatch
